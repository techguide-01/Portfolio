Course Description

Field Study 1 - Observations of Teaching – Learning Actual School Environment

This is the first experiential course, which will immerse a future teacher in an actual classroom situation and learning environment where direct observation of teaching-learning episodes that focus on the application of educational theories learned in content and pedagogy courses will be made. Observation on learner’s behavior, motivation, and teacher’s strategies of teaching, classroom management, and assessment in learning among others shall be given emphasis. This course is meant to assist field study students in verifying their conduct in the actual learning environment. It will necessitate them recognizing possible ways to facilitate learning while considering the learner's various stages of development and social surroundings.
